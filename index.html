About the Camp

Develop Your Game.
Find Your Confidence.

The Bothell Girls Basketball Camp is a 3-day intensive designed to help young athletes sharpen their skills, build basketball IQ, and grow their love for the game. Whether you're stepping on the court for the first time or preparing for your school team, this camp meets you where you are.

Directed by the Bothell High School Girls Basketball coaching staff, campers will receive the same quality instruction and attention that has built one of KingCo's top programs. Daily schedules are packed with skill work, competition, and individual evaluation — all in a supportive, high-energy environment.

Skill Development

Shooting, ball handling, footwork & offensive moves

Team Play

Daily games, team competitions & learning to play together

Individual Attention

Personal evaluation and position-specific coaching

Mindset

Goal setting, confidence building & motivation techniques

What You'll Learn

Curriculum Built for
Real Improvement

01

Shooting Mechanics

Form shooting, catch-and-shoot, off-the-dribble pull-ups, and free throw technique.

02

Ball Handling

Dribble moves, change of pace, crossovers, and attacking off the bounce.

03

Offensive Footwork

Triple threat, jab steps, pivoting, and creating space without the ball.

04

Defensive Fundamentals

Stance, closeouts, help defense positioning, and on-ball pressure.

05

Passing & Court Vision

Chest, bounce, and overhead passes. Reading the floor and making smart decisions.

06

Game Situations

1-on-1, 3-on-3, and 5-on-5 play with coaching feedback in real time.

07

Rebounding & Hustle

Boxing out, pursuit of the ball, and competing for every possession.

08

Leadership & Mindset

Goal setting, positive self-talk, being a great teammate, and competing with confidence.
